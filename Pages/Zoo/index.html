About the project

"ZooMelaka" Zoological Manangement System

As Per The Second Year (2021) Second Semester Industrial Training Project @ Sri Lanka Institute of Information Technology - Malabe, Sri Lanka, A Zoological Management System Was Implemented With MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, NodeJS) For ZooMelaka, Malaysia.
Implemented Basic Create, Retrieve, Update, Delete And Search Functionalities.
A-Team Effort Consisting Of A Group Of 8 Members.
